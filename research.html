SUMMARY

I'm a third year bioengineering and EECS (electrical engineering and computer science) student at the University of California, Berkeley. My interests lie within the intersection of these two fields for the development of medical devices. Through the device industry, I aim to overcome unmet clinical needs in low resource settings by working closely with clinicians, engineers, and scientists.

My previous projects have focused on the product development process, exploring potential solutions from ideation to prototype. By defining a need and understanding how users are affected by it, I aim to create a device with the optimal form, fit, and function.
